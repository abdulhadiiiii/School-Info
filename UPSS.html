USMAN PUBLIC SCHOOL SYSTEM

Usman Public School, renowned for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development, stands as a beacon of quality education and nurturing growth. Established with a visionary approach, the school has consistently upheld its reputation for fostering a stimulating learning environment, encouraging critical thinking, and promoting values that empower students to become responsible global citizens. With a strong emphasis on innovation, character building, and a comprehensive curriculum, Usman Public School has emerged as a distinguished institution dedicated to shaping young minds and preparing them for the challenges of an ever-evolving world.

PRE-SCHOOL SYSTEM

In pre-school, children learn through direct hands-on experience with people, objects, events,and ideas in the light of Islamic teachings. It is ensured that their experiences are active pleasant and safe. Trained teachers who understand child development and are knowledgeable about the important areas of learning in the pre-school years offer guidance and support in a friendly and peaceful environment. The system prepares the child to read and write at a very early age.

ELEMENTARY SCHOOL SYSTEM

The elementary school system lays the foundation for more structured learning experiences.

OUR FACILITIES

At Usman Public School, we take pride in providing top-notch facilities that create a conducive and enjoyable learning environment for our students. Our campus is equipped with modern classrooms that are comfortable and equipped with the latest technology to enhance the learning experience. Additionally, we offer well-equipped science and computer labs that enable hands-on exploration and experimentation. Our library is a treasure trove of knowledge, housing a wide range of books and resources to support both academic and personal growth. For physical development, we have spacious sports fields and a gymnasium, encouraging a healthy and active lifestyle. At Usman Public School, we believe that the right facilities can greatly enhance the educational journey, and we are dedicated to offering the best to our students.

WELL-EQUIPPED SCIENCE LABORATORIES

The science laboratories are equipped with necessary material for Matriculation, Intermediate and GCE 'O' level ATP examination. The O-level Campuses offer these facilities according to the standards recommended by the Cambridge board. Separate laboratories have been set up for Intermediate students.

LIBRARIES

The Usman Public School System libraries have a wide collection of books for both students and teachers. Each library is continuously replenished with the latest books to ensure the establishment of a reading culture. A huge variety of text books and reference books are available for the students to select from. Monthly journals and magazines are also stocked along with teacher's resource material.

WELL-EQUIPPED COMPUTER LABORATORIES

The computer laboratories are equipped with LCD screens and audio- visual facilities. All students are given hands on computer training. UPSS emphasizes education through computers by encouraging students to carry out web-based research work for various class projects and assignments.

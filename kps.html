Happy Palace Grammar School



Happy Palace Group of Schools were established towards the end of the twentieth century (1982). With a rich history of thirty years (40 years), the School leads as a symbol of educational aspiration for students with a special purpose of providing quality education at an affordable price. At a time, when government allocation and spending was minimal on the education sector, HPGS established itself as one of the leading education providers in the city.

Courses We Offer

From Class 1 to Class 11, Learning Modules are available as a component of the Program of Studies. These courses give students the chance to explore and develop their passions outside the realm of traditional academics. An all-around education depends on the Learning Modules. Every year, each student takes one module. Students may take the same module for multiple years or alternate between various modules each year. The time for all learning modules is during the academic day.

Our Facilities

Happy Palace Grammar School boasts state-of-the-art facilities that provide an enriching and comprehensive learning environment for students. The campus is equipped with modern classrooms, well-equipped science and computer labs to foster hands-on learning, a well-stocked library to encourage exploration and research, and spacious sports facilities to promote physical activity and teamwork. The school takes pride in its commitment to nurturing holistic development, with facilities designed to cater to the academic, creative, and physical growth of every student.

HPGS Science Lab

The science lab at Happy Palace Grammar School is a dynamic hub of exploration and discovery, where students delve into the fascinating realms of science. Equipped with modern instruments and resources, the lab offers a hands-on experience that enhances understanding and ignites curiosity. From conducting intricate experiments to unraveling complex theories, students engage in experiential learning that goes beyond textbooks.

Merit Scholarship

Happy Palace Grammar School takes pride in recognizing and rewarding academic excellence through its merit scholarship program. Designed to celebrate exceptional achievements, these scholarships are awarded to students who consistently demonstrate outstanding academic performance. The school's commitment to nurturing talent and encouraging intellectual growth is exemplified by these scholarships, which not only provide financial assistance but also inspire students to strive for their best.

Sports Ground

Nestled in a picturesque landscape, the grounds of Happy Palace Grammar School offer an inviting and serene space for students to thrive both academically and personally. Lush green lawns provide ample room for outdoor activities and relaxation, while vibrant gardens create a soothing ambiance. The meticulously maintained sports fields encourage a spirit of healthy competition and physical well-being.

Overview

Curriculum

We offer a curriculum that meets the needs of individual learners and allows them every opportunity for success. The curriculum is broad, rich and flexible. It provides all pupils with a firm grasp of the fundamentals whilst allowing an appropriate degree of specialization and depth. A focus on progress in English and Mathematics is at the heart of our curriculum providing a strong foundation for success in all other subjects and in later life. Innovative teaching and learning strategies As a learning organisation, we try to stay up to date with the rapidly changing global environment in order to provide our students with a cutting-edge education that will prepare them for the future. This is why our curriculum and instructional strategies are always changing and developing to be at par with the latest advancements. Our students become lifelong, independent learners and assured leaders of the future thanks to our innovative teaching method.
